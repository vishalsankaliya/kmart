Cancellation & Returns

Order Cancellation

You can cancel your order before it is shipped. Once the order is shipped, cancellation will no longer be possible.

  • Go to My Orders
  • Select the order you want to cancel
  • Click Cancel Order
  • Refund will be processed within 5-7 business days

Return Policy

If you receive a damaged or incorrect product, you can request a return within 7 days of delivery.

  • Product must be unused
  • Original packaging should be available
  • Invoice must be included

Refund Information

After the returned product is received and inspected, the refund will be credited to your original payment method.

  • UPI / Card payments: 5-7 days
  • Cash on Delivery orders: Bank transfer

Need Help?

If you need assistance with cancellation or returns, please visit our Contact Us page.
