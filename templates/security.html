Security Policy

Your safety and data protection are important to us.

1. Secure Transactions

All payments on K-Mart are processed through secure payment gateways. We use encryption technologies to protect your payment information.

2. Data Protection

Your personal information such as name, email, and address is stored securely. We never sell or share your personal information with unauthorized third parties.

3. Account Safety

Users are responsible for maintaining the confidentiality of their account login details. Please choose a strong password and do not share it with others.

4. Fraud Prevention

K-Mart continuously monitors transactions to detect and prevent fraudulent activities to ensure a safe shopping environment.

5. Reporting Security Issues

If you notice any suspicious activity or security issues on our website, please contact our support team immediately.
