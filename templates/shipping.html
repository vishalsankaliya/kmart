Shipping Information

1. Shipping Time

Orders are usually delivered within 3-7 business days depending on your location. Metro cities may receive orders faster.

2. Shipping Charges

• Free shipping on orders above ₹499.
• ₹40 delivery charge for orders below ₹499.

3. Order Tracking

Once your order is shipped, you will receive a tracking ID via email or SMS. You can track your order from the My Orders section.

4. Delayed Delivery

Sometimes delivery may take longer due to weather conditions, holidays, or courier delays. If your order is delayed, please contact our support team.

5. Contact Support

If you have any questions about shipping, please visit our Contact Us page.
