Frequently Asked Questions

1. How do I place an order?

Browse products, click on the product you want, add it to your cart, and proceed to checkout to place your order.

2. What payment methods are accepted?

We accept Credit Card, Debit Card, UPI, Net Banking and Cash on Delivery.

3. How can I track my order?

After placing an order, you can track it from the "My Orders" section in your account.

4. What is the return policy?

You can return most products within 7 days of delivery if they are unused and in original packaging.

5. How can I contact customer support?

You can visit our Contact Us page or email us at support@kmart.com.
