Our Return Policy

At K Mart, customer satisfaction is our priority. If you are not completely satisfied with your purchase, you may return the product according to the terms below.

1. Return Eligibility

  • Items must be returned within 7 days of delivery.
  • Product must be unused and in original packaging.
  • All tags, manuals, and accessories must be included.

2. Non-Returnable Items

  • Used or damaged products
  • Personal care items
  • Gift cards or downloadable products

3. Refund Process

Once we receive and inspect the returned item, your refund will be processed. The refund will be credited to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.

4. Return Shipping

Customers may need to pay return shipping charges unless the product was received damaged or incorrect.

5. How to Request a Return

  1. Go to your order history.
  2. Select the product you want to return.
  3. Click the Request Return button.
  4. Our support team will contact you within 24 hours.

Need Help?

If you have questions about returns, please contact our support team through the Contact Us page.
