Payments Help

Information about payment methods, refunds and payment issues.

Accepted Payment Methods

  • Credit Card (Visa, MasterCard)
  • Debit Card
  • UPI (Google Pay, PhonePe, Paytm)
  • Net Banking
  • Cash on Delivery (COD)

Payment Failed?

If your payment fails, please check the following:

  • Check your internet connection
  • Ensure your card details are correct
  • Make sure your bank has approved the transaction
  • Try another payment method

Refund Policy

If your payment was successful but the order was not placed, the amount will be refunded to your original payment method within 5–7 business days.

Secure Payments

K-Mart uses secure payment gateways and SSL encryption to ensure your payment details are safe and protected.

Need More Help?

If you still have payment issues, please contact our support team.
